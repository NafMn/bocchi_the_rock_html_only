Bocchi The Rock

Bocchi the Rock! is a band comedy 4-koma manga series by Aki Hamazi for story and illustration. The manga series serialized in Manga Time Kirara MAX by Houbunsha since February 2018 issue and collected four volumes in tankōbon format. It become a popular series for its successor of K-ON! and ranked 8th place in the Next Manga Awards 2019. An anime series produced by CloverWorks and premiered on October 9, 2022.
